## Project Overview

This project addresses the problem of insufficient power supply and battery replacement in traditional smart door locks in remote areas by designing a self-powered smart door lock integrating piezoelectric ceramics and electromagnetic induction technology.

## Technical Approach

- Innovative door handle rotation power generation
- Floor-based step power generation system
- Efficiently converts mechanical energy from daily door handle rotation and floor stepping into electrical energy
